About Us

Welcome to the Victorian Immunobullous Service, a specialized initiative led by Dermatologist Associate Professor Laura Scardamaglia and Oral Mucosal physician Dr. Tami Yap at the Royal Melbourne Hospital.

Our Mission

Dedicated to advancing the understanding and treatment of immunobullous diseases, our service is committed to providing specialized care and fostering collaboration among clinicians across various disciplines. Under the leadership of Associate Professor Laura Scardamaglia and Dr. Tami Yap, our team is focused on delivering comprehensive and cutting-edge solutions for patients grappling with these challenging conditions.

Our Team

Associate Professor Laura Scardamaglia: A distinguished Dermatologist, Professor Scardamaglia brings a wealth of expertise to the forefront of our service. Her dedication to advancing dermatological care has positioned her as a leader in the field.

Dr. Tami Yap: As an Oral Mucosal physician, Dr. Yap specializes in addressing the unique challenges presented by immunobullous diseases affecting the oral cavity. Her commitment to patient care and research is instrumental in shaping our service.

Collaboration

At the Victorian Immunobullous Service, we recognize the complexity of immunobullous diseases and understand the importance of a multidisciplinary approach. We are eager to establish connections with clinicians who share our dedication to this specialized field.
